Connect Gately with Mailchimp to send automated welcome emails, transactional messages, and marketing campaigns to your users.

## Quick Start

Get started with Gately and Mailchimp in 3 simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Mailchimp Account">
    Authorize Gately to access your Mailchimp account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Settings">
    Choose your audience and configure your email settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the Integration">
    Activate the integration to start sending emails.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Connect Your Mailchimp Account

1. Go to [https://app.usegately.com/dashboard/settings/integrations](https://app.usegately.com/dashboard/settings/integrations)
2. Select **Mailchimp** from the list of integrations
3. Click **Connect** and follow the prompts to authorize Gately to access your Mailchimp account.

### Step 2: Configure Your Settings

1. Once connected, you will be redirected back to the Gately dashboard.
2. Select the Mailchimp **audience** you want to sync your users with.
3. Configure the **welcome email** and other transactional email settings.

### Step 3: Enable the Integration

1.  After configuring your settings, click **Enable Integration**.
2.  Gately will now automatically sync new users to your Mailchimp audience and send emails as configured.

<Note>
You can customize the content of your emails in your Mailchimp account. Gately simply triggers the emails to be sent.
</Note>
