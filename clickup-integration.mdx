## Connecting to ClickUp

To connect your ClickUp workspace to Gately, follow these steps:

1. **Navigate to the Integrations Page:** From your Gately dashboard, go to the "Integrations" page.
2. **Select ClickUp:** Find the ClickUp integration and click "Connect."
3. **Authorize Gately:** You will be redirected to ClickUp to authorize Gately to access your workspace.
4. **Select Your Workspace:** Choose the ClickUp workspace you want to connect to Gately.
5. **Start Migrating:** Once connected, you can start migrating your tasks from ClickUp to Gately.
