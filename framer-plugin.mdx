# The Gately Framer Plugin

The Gately plugin for Framer is the fastest and easiest way to bring powerful Gately features into your projects. It's designed to streamline your workflow, allowing you to build, manage, and deploy faster than ever.

### Key Features

- **Drag-and-Drop Components:** No more copying and pasting. Access the entire Gately component library directly in Framer and simply drag and drop components onto your canvas.
- **Automatic Setup:** The plugin handles the technical setup for you. It programmatically installs the Gately JavaScript snippet into your project, so you can get started right away.
- **In-Plugin Management:** Manage your Gately projects without leaving Framer. Access your dashboard, view members, and adjust settings, all from a convenient panel.

### Get Started in Seconds

1. **Install the Plugin:** Get the latest version of our Framer plugin from the link below.
2. **Connect Your Account:** Link the plugin to your Gately account.
3. **Build:** Drag components onto your canvas and start building your project.
