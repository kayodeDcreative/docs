# Framer Plugin for E-commerce

We're excited to introduce a powerful new feature for our Framer plugin: **E-commerce Product Imports**. Now, you can seamlessly import your entire product catalog directly into the Framer CMS, making it easier than ever to design and launch beautiful, custom e-commerce sites.

### Key Features

- **Effortless Product Sync:** Import products from your e-commerce platform to the Framer CMS with just a few clicks.
- **Design Freedom:** Use Framer's powerful design tools to create unique and engaging product pages, all powered by your own product data.
- **Streamlined Workflow:** Eliminate the need for manual data entry and keep your product information consistent across platforms.

### How It Works

1. **Install the Plugin:** Get the latest version of our Framer plugin from the link below.
2. **Connect Your Store:** Link the plugin to your e-commerce store to access your product data.
3. **Import Products:** Select the products you want to import, and the plugin will automatically create corresponding entries in the Framer CMS.
4. **Design and Publish:** With your products in the CMS, you can now design your e-commerce site in Framer and publish it to the web.
