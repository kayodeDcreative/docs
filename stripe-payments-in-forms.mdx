# Accept Payments in Forms with Stripe

We are excited to announce a powerful new feature for our Form Builder: Stripe integration. Now you can securely and easily accept payments, donations, and subscriptions directly within your forms. This integration is designed to be seamless, providing a smooth and professional experience for both you and your customers.

## Why Use Stripe in Your Forms?

- **Increased Conversion Rates:** By keeping users on your site to complete their payments, you reduce friction and increase the likelihood of them completing the form.
- **Simplified Workflow:** Manage all your form submissions and payments in one place, streamlining your administrative tasks.
- **Enhanced Customer Trust:** Offering a secure and professional payment experience builds trust and confidence with your audience.

## Getting Started

To start accepting payments in your forms, simply drag the new "Stripe Payment" element into your form in the Form Builder, and follow the on-screen instructions to connect your Stripe account.
