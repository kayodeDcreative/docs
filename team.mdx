## Role Permissions

Gately allows you to invite your team members and assign them granular permissions to ensure everyone has the right level of access to your project.

| Role    | Access Level          | Description                               | Permissions                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| :------ | :-------------------- | :---------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Owner   | Full Access           | Complete control over the project       | • All Admin permissions<br/>• Manage billing and subscriptions<br/>• Delete project<br/>• Transfer ownership                                                                                             |
| Admin   | Manage & Configure    | Can manage team and configure all project settings | • All Member permissions<br/>• Invite and remove team members<br/>• Edit project settings and branding<br/>• Manage integrations (ClickUp, Slack, etc.)<br/>• Configure SSO and email settings<br/>• Delete forms, tasks, and content |
| Member  | Create & Edit Content | Can work with content but cannot change settings  | • View all project content<br/>• View forms and submissions<br/>• View helpdesk tickets<br/>• View tasks and analytics<br/>• Export data<br/>• Cannot edit or delete                                       |
| Viewer  | Read-Only Access      | Can only view content and reports       | • View all project content<br/>• View forms and submissions<br/>• View helpdesk tickets<br/>• View tasks and analytics<br/>• Cannot edit, delete, or export                                              |
