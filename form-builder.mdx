# Gately Form Builder

Our advanced Form Builder is a complete solution for creating and managing any type of form your project needs. From simple contact forms to complex, multi-step surveys with payment processing, our intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to build without code.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="40+ Form Elements" icon="box-select">
    Build any form you can imagine with a vast library of over 40 elements, including text fields, file uploads, ratings, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Step Forms" icon="layers">
    Break up long forms into user-friendly steps to improve completion rates and user experience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conditional Logic" icon="git-branch">
    Create dynamic forms that show or hide fields, steps, or questions based on user input for a personalized experience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment Processing" icon="credit-card">
    Securely accept payments and donations directly within your forms by integrating with Stripe.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SEO Optimized" icon="search">
    Ensure your forms are easily discoverable. Our forms are built with SEO best practices to help you rank higher.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flexible Embedding" icon="code">
    Embed your forms anywhere with a simple iframe, or integrate them directly into your website or application.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Form Management and Analytics

- **Submission Tracking:** View, manage, and export all your form submissions from a centralized dashboard.
- **Performance Analytics:** Gain insights into your form's performance with detailed analytics on views, submissions, and conversion rates.
- **Custom Notifications:** Set up email notifications to be alerted instantly of new submissions.

## Getting Started

Ready to build your first form? Head over to the Form Builder in your Gately dashboard and start exploring the powerful features available to you.
