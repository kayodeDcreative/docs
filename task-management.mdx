## Task Management

Gately's task management system provides you with the tools to manage your tasks efficiently. You can also import your tasks from ClickUp by connecting your ClickUp workspace in our integrations page.

### Features
- **Manage Tasks:** Create, edit, and delete tasks to keep your projects on track.
- **Import from ClickUp:** Easily migrate your tasks from ClickUp to Gately.
- **Seamless Integration:** Connect your ClickUp workspace to Gately for a smooth task management experience.
