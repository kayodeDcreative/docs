## Quick Start

Get started with Gately and Slack in 3 simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Slack Workspace">
    Authorize Gately to access your Slack workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Channel">
    Select the channel where you want to receive notifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Notifications">
    Choose the events you want to be notified about.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Connect Your Slack Workspace

1. Go to [https://app.usegately.com/dashboard/settings/integrations](https://app.usegately.com/dashboard/settings/integrations)
2. Select **Slack** from the list of integrations
3. Click **Connect** and follow the prompts to authorize Gately to access your Slack workspace.

### Step 2: Choose Your Channel

1. Once connected, you will be redirected back to the Gately dashboard.
2. Select the Slack **channel** where you want to receive notifications.
3. You can choose a public or private channel.

### Step 3: Configure Your Notifications

1.  Choose the events you want to be notified about:
    -   New form submissions
    -   New member signups
    -   New helpdesk tickets
    -   New helpdesk messages
2.  After configuring your settings, click **Enable Integration**.

<Note>
You can set up multiple Slack integrations to send different notifications to different channels.
</Note>
